Questions

Still wondering?

Is this an AI model writing my schedule?
+
No — and that's a feature. PrepVeda uses a deterministic constraint solver. Same inputs, same plan every time. No hallucinations, no random rewrites.
Does it work for my specific exam?
+
PrepVeda is exam-agnostic. If you can break your prep into subjects and chapters with effort estimates, it works — board exams, competitive entrance, professional certs, semester finals.
What happens when I fall behind or miss a day?
+
Re-run the planner in Intake mode. You can keep the previous plan structure or let it re-optimise around what's left. Feasibility re-checks immediately.
Can I edit the generated schedule by hand?
+
Yes. Drag tasks across days, mark things complete, or add ad-hoc sessions. Manual edits don't break the plan.
Is there a free plan?
+
Yes. The free plan includes full planner access, subjects, calendar and schedule. No card required.
